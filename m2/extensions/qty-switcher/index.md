# Quantity Switcher

The module adds quantity switch buttons to the product and shopping cart pages. Allows customers to change the quantity of products to add to the cart.

![Quantity Switcher](/images/m2/qty-switcher/qty-switcher.png)

### Configuration

Module can be enabled in `Stores > Configuration > Swissup > Quantity Switcher > General > Enabled`.

You can choose the `Quantity switcher type`: Arrows or Dropdown.

You can configure it for shopping cart with `Enabled on Cart Page`.
